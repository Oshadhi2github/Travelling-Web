ACCOMMODATION // HERITANCE KANDALAMA

Perched on the edge of a cliff this award-winning property offers a stylish commune with nature only 15 minutes from dambulla town. set amid extensively landscaped gardens and surrounded by verdant wilderness, heritance kandalama is perfectly situated for exploring sri lanka's cultural triangle. Two of the country's most famous historical sitees-the dambulla rock temple and the sigiriya rock temple are located close to the hotel grounds and so too is the resplendent kandalama lake which is accessible by foot or on horseback. rooms of heritance kandalama are contemporary and minimal, and guests can enjoy vast, undisturbed views of the forest from their private balconies. Recreational facilities include as infinity pool and a spa to release your stress while you vacation in style in sri lanka.
